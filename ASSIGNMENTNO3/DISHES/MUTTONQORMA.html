MUTTON QORMA

Mutton qorma is a rich and flavorful meat dish originating from the Indian subcontinent. It is a popular dish that combines tender mutton (goat or lamb) with a creamy and aromatic gravy made with various spices and yogurt.Mutton qorma is typically served with naan, roti, or rice. It's a delicious and satisfying dish that is enjoyed in various parts of the Indian subcontinent. The flavors of the spices and slow-cooked mutton come together to create a rich and aromatic culinary experience.
