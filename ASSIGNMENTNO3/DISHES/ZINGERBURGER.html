ZINGER BURGER

A Zinger burger is a popular fast food item offered by KFC (Kentucky Fried Chicken), a well-known international fast-food chain. It is known for its spicy and flavorful taste.A typical Zinger burger consists of a breaded and deep-fried chicken fillet that is marinated with a blend of spices, including paprika, cayenne pepper, and black pepper. The chicken fillet is often coated in a crispy.
