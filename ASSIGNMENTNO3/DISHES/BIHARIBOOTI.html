BIHARI BOOTI

Chicken boti is the generic term used for chicken pieces taken from thigh and drumstick parts of the hen. Boti in general, is the boneless piece of meat whether it is mutton, beef or chicken. Boti refers to the small bite size piece of meat. Chicken boti is used to make various chicken dishes such as chicken manchurian, chicken shashlik, chicken bihariboti or chicken jalfrezi recipe.
