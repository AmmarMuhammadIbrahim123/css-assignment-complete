CHICKEN HAANDI

Chicken Handi translates to chicken in clay pot. Traditionally this curry is made in a terrocota pot which imparts its unique flavour to this dish. Handi Chicken dish is a traditional North Indian style curry, slow-cooked in a copper or earthen clay pot (called handi) with aromatic spices, yogurt, cream, and fragrant herbs. For a home cook like us, we can use any heavy bottom pot or pan instead of the traditional clay pot.
