BEEF BIRYANI

Biryani has two main components: fluffy, fragrant basmati rice and a delicious saucy beef (or your choice of protein) curry or masala. AND, it’s super delicious.It’s also what I would say the equivalent of Sunday roast in Pakistan, as it’s almost always made once a week either on Friday (mostly) or Sunday in most households in Pakistani. And usually when Biryani is in the making, Zarda a sweet rice recipe is also made on the same day as dessert. It is a lovely and popular combination.
