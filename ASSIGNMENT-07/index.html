SAYLANI WELFARE  INTERNATIONAL  TRUST

As of my last update in September 2021, Saylani Welfare International Trust is a well-known charitable organization based in Pakistan. It was founded in 1999 by Maulana Bashir Ahmed Farooqui, a prominent religious scholar and social worker. Saylani Welfare International Trust is dedicated to providing various humanitarian services and assistance to the underprivileged and marginalized communities in Pakistan. The organization's activities cover a wide range of areas, including:

1. Food Distribution:

Saylani Welfare International Trust runs a large-scale food distribution program, providing free meals to the needy, especially during times of crises and emergencies.

2. Medical Facilities:

Saylani Welfare International Trust operates medical clinics and provides healthcare services to those who cannot afford proper medical treatment.

3. Clean Water Projects:

The organization works to provide clean drinking water to communities where access to clean water is limited.

4. Orphan Care:

Saylani Welfare International Trust runs orphanages and provides support and care to orphaned children.

5. Vocational Training:

The trust offers vocational training programs to equip individuals with skills that can help them find employment and improve their livelihoods.

6. Disaster Relief:

During natural disasters or emergencies, the organization engages in relief efforts to provide aid to affected communities.

7. Education:

The trust also focuses on promoting education among disadvantaged children by establishing schools and providing scholarships and educational resources.
