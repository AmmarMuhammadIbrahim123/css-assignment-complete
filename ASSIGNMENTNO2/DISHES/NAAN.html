NAAN

Naan is a popular type of bread that is commonly found in Indian, Pakistani, and other South Asian cuisines. It is a soft and fluffy bread that is typically cooked in a tandoor (clay oven), but it can also be made on a stovetop or in a regular oven.Naan is best served warm and can be enjoyed with various dishes such as curries, kebabs, or as a side with dips like hummus or raita. Its soft texture and slightly chewy nature make it a delicious accompaniment to many meals.
